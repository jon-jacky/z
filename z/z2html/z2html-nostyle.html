Z2HTML (No Stylesheet Version)

Back to the Z2HTML home page.

At first I was reluctant to use stylesheets because older browsers do not support them. An early version of z2html.sed controlled the appearance and indentation of the Z text using font face and blockquote tags instead.

Here is z2html-nostyle.sed.

Here is the test page, translated by the no-stylesheet version.

Later I decided that stylesheets were too useful to do without. Few people are still using those old browsers, and for those that do, the appearance is adequate.

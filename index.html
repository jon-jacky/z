Z notation

for writing formal specifications of computing systems.

This web site was made by Jonathan Jacky at the University of Washington starting around 1997. It is archived here for historical interest.

This page is new. All of the other pages at this site are at least twenty years old and have not been revised. Links to pages outside this site might not work, and will not be fixed.

These are the recommended entry points to this site:
